BBQ Chicken

Ingredients:

  1. 350 gm chicken
  2. 1/2 cup hung curd
  3. 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  4. 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  5. 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  6. 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  7. 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper
  9. 1 teaspoon garam masala powder

Common Allergens:

Milk, Dairy, Garlic, Cumin, Coriander, cardamom
